Our Mission

Serving Those Who Served Us

Founded by U.S. Army veteran Clyde Fleming Jr., CFJR SAFE TRAVELS provides reliable, accessible transportation that breaks down barriers for veterans and seniors in the Metro-Atlanta area.

"After serving our country, I wanted to continue serving my community. Everyone deserves safe, dignified transportation — regardless of mobility challenges."

— Clyde Fleming, Jr.

Service-Disabled U.S. Army Veteran • Founder & CEO
